What is this site?

This site helps with exploring professional disc golf events with our interactive platform. Easily filter tournaments by tier, location, or date, and dive into key stats like participant numbers, travel distances, and local vs. out-of-state attendance. Find the most popular events and click to view detailed insights about each one. Whether you're a fan, player, or organizer, this site helps you stay informed and connected to the growing world of disc golf.

Who is StatMando?

StatMando is disc golf's leading stats platform, offering in-depth player profiles, offical and custom rankings, weekly tournament recaps, and unique head-to-head comparisons. Whether you're a fan, player, or analyst, StatMando helps you explore performance trends, field strength, and historical data through powerful tools and visuals, bringing the story of disc golf to life through stats.

Who is the PDGA?

The Professional Disc Golf Association (PDGA) is the global governing body for disc golf. It oversees the offical rules, player ratings, event standards, and world rankings while supporting the growth of the game through memberships, tournaments, and education. The PDGA also manages elections, approves disc and equipment, and provides resources for players, organizers, and fans to stay connected with the sport's latest developments.
